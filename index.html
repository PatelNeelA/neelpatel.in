A front-end developer specializes in creating the visual and interactive aspects of a website or application that users directly engage with. They use languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with frameworks and libraries such as React.js, to build responsive and aesthetically pleasing interfaces. Their role involves translating design mockups into functional code, ensuring cross-browser compatibility, optimizing performance, and focusing on user experience. Front-end developers work closely with designers and back-end developers to deliver seamless, engaging, and intuitive digital experiences
